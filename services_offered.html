Natal Chart Reading

A comprehensive analysis of your birth chart, revealing insights into your personality, strengths, weaknesses, and life path based on planetary positions at the time of your birth.

Horoscope Matching

An essential service for marriage, where the compatibility between two individuals is assessed by comparing their birth charts. Horoscope matching ensures harmony, understanding, and mutual respect in relationships.

Daily Horoscope

Receive daily insights and predictions based on your zodiac sign. This service provides guidance for the day ahead, helping you make informed decisions and avoid potential challenges.

Muhurta (Auspicious Timing)

A specialized service to determine the most auspicious time for important events such as weddings, starting a business, or buying property. Muhurta ensures that the cosmic energies are favorable for success and prosperity.

Gemstone Consultation

Based on your birth chart, this service recommends specific gemstones that enhance positive planetary energies and mitigate the effects of malefic planets, bringing balance and protection to your life.

Name Correction (Numerology)

A numerology-based service that suggests alterations to your name for greater success, fortune, and personal alignment with your destiny. It balances your name with your birth numbers for enhanced positivity.

Vastu Consultation

Vastu Shastra consultations help align your home or office space with cosmic energies. By following Vastu principles, you create a balanced and harmonious environment, promoting peace, health, and prosperity.
